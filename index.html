For me, teaching is a calling. From my earliest childhood memories,
I remember loving to play “school.”
This love of the entire process of learning
has followed me throughout the changes in my career path.
In any role, regardless of whether
I am acting in the formal roles of “instructor” or “student,”
I seek to learn and to teach.
